How This Proctored Exam Works
Before you start, please read these instructions:
  • You must allow camera and microphone access when prompted.
  • On computers (Windows, macOS, Linux, Chrome OS), when the browser asks you must choose Entire Screen. Window or tab sharing is not accepted and the exam will not proceed without whole-screen capture. On phones and tablets (Android/iOS/iPad), this full-screen/system capture step is omitted so taking the exam is possible; monitoring still uses your camera and microphone.
  • Dual monitors (desktop only): If you have multiple monitors, choose "Entire Screen" so all displays can be reviewed. Avoid moving the exam window between monitors with shortcuts.
  • Do not switch tabs/apps, resize the exam window, or exit fullscreen while taking the exam.
  • Copy, paste, and cut (Ctrl+C, Ctrl+V, Ctrl+X) are disabled during the exam. These shortcuts will be blocked and may be flagged as violations.
  • Keep your face clearly visible to the camera at all times and avoid using your phone or other devices.
  • Camera and microphone are recorded subject to institutional policy (often deleted after retention). On supported desktop browsers, entire-screen recordings may also be collected; typically not collected on phones/tablets.
