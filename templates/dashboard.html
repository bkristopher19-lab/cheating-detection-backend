Admin dashboard guide

Administrators oversee the entire proctoring platform: roster and class setup, oversight of assessments, operational metrics, and data retention policies.

  • 1
    Sign inUse your administrator account from the institutional login screen.
  • 2
    Review this dashboardOverview
    Use summary cards for students, exams, sessions, alerts, and completion signals. Drill into tables with Exam and Section filters.
  • 3
    Manage usersOpen Users to add or edit roles, approve accounts, or adjust finalized scores where policy allows.
  • 4
    Maintain classesIn Classes, create courses and sections, keep join codes current, and assign teachers or proctors.
  • 5
    Manage examsStaff use Exams for scheduling, question pools, durations, and window dates linked to sections.
  • 6
    Monitoring and reportsUse Reports for session timelines, violations, recordings, and shared links for formal review chains.
  • 7
    System logsAudit
    The System logs table lists authenticated sign-ins and key session milestones with timestamps for accountability.
  • 8
    Danger zoneDeletion
    Delete ALL Reports removes stored sessions (and optionally results). Use only with institutional clearance; consider archival policy first.
  • 9
    LogoutEnd your administrator session from the profile menu when work is finished.
