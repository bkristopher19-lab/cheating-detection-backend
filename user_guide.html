Student Dashboard Guide

Step-by-step usage
  • 1
    Login to the system
    Enter your institutional credentials on the login page and complete authentication.
  • 2
    Access the student dashboard
    Upon success, open your student dashboard listing available classes and exams.
  • 3
    Start exam session
    Select the scheduled assessment and begin the session within the permitted time window.
  • 4
    Enable camera Important
    When prompted, grant browser permission so the webcam stream can power AI monitoring continuously.
  • 5
    Follow exam rules while being monitored
    Remain visible, minimize distractions, and comply with syllabus integrity requirements until submission.
  • 6
    Receive alerts if suspicious behavior is detected Alerts
    On-screen prompts may advise you when the analyzer flags deviations so you may self-correct before escalation.
  • 7
    Submit exam
    Finish all items, verify answers if allowed, then submit before the countdown expires.
  • 8
    Logout
    Use the logout control to securely end your session, especially on shared devices.

Proctor Dashboard Guide

Step-by-step usage
  • 1
    Login to the system
    Authenticate with authorized proctor credentials through the institutional login gateway.
  • 2
    Access proctor dashboard
    Open the supervisory overview that aggregates active examinations and learner states.
  • 3
    View active student sessions
    Identify cohorts presently sitting the exam through the roster or session widgets.
  • 4
    Monitor students via camera feed Camera
    Select a candidate tile to inspect the live ingest stream synced with analytic overlays.
  • 5
    Receive system-generated alerts Alerts
    Observe priority-ranked notifications surfaced when rule engines detect policy-relevant events.
  • 6
    Review suspicious activity logs
    Open chronological incident records to corroborate context before adjudication.
  • 7
    Take action (flag/warning/report)
    Annotate incidents, notify the student formally, or escalate through prescribed reporting rails.
  • 8
    Logout
    Terminate the supervisory session from the profile or session menu upon completion.
